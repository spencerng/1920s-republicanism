Group Thesis

While Harding and Coolidge allowed business to prosper, their republican administrations ultimately caused a greater economic divide in the United States by discouraging foreign trade with high tariffs, distributing wealth unevenly among social classes and businesses, and managing government resources inefficiently, leading to the Great Depression.
